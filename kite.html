My journey with kitesurfing began in 2004 at Lake Miedwie in Poland. It was a long and challenging path, learning on my own with my father from VHS tapes.

In 2005, I spent my first summer in Tarifa and instantly fell in love with the place. I was kiting 5–6 hours a day and spending all my time on the beach.

I trained across Europe and later moved to Tarifa permanently in 2011. Since 2017, I’ve been teaching at Alex Pastor Kite Club, helping students progress with safety and confidence.
