I caught my first wave in 2008 at Balneario, Tarifa — and from that moment, I knew I wanted to live close to the ocean.

In 2009, I had the amazing opportunity to join the Erasmus program in Las Palmas de Gran Canaria. I surfed for 10 months straight — and got paid for it, haha!

Thanks to seasonal jobs, I was able to travel around the world and surf great waves in Morocco, Portugal, Peru, the Canary Islands, and Bali. I learned a lot, and every windless day in Tarifa became a chance to improve in the water.

I love surfing waves of all sizes, on all kinds of boards. On flat days, I focus on technique through surfskate, mobility training, slackline, and balance board exercises. For me, surfing is more than a sport — it's a way of life.
